What is Rune ?

Rune stones are used as tools of divination-a way to predict one future. The word Rune means secret or mystery. The word Rune means a secret and mystery. Runes are a connection between a person their intuition and the power of the symbol contained on the Rune. Runes are stone with ancient symbols curved on them. These symbols are base on the Germanic alphabets which was used for writing and divination. Runes are thought to have magical powers including the power to protect and heal.

They were probably transformed from the tarot and the Chinese Book of Changes and were used as the I Ching of the Vikings at the time. From the beginning the runes had a ritual function for divination and invocation of higher powers that could affect the lives and happiness of everyone.
