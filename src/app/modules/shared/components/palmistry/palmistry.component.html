What is Palmistry Reading?

Palmistry, also known as chiromancy, is the ancient practice of interpreting the lines, shapes, and features of the hand. It provides deep insights into a person’s character, health, career prospects, love life, and spiritual path based on the unique patterns found in their palms.

The palmistry reading will be done on the right hand, and the left hand will be used for reference. The right hand is considered the active hand, representing your current life and choices, while the left hand reflects your potential and inherited traits.

The reading will focus on the major lines: the Heart Line, Head Line, and Life Line, along with the mounts and other features of your palm.

The Heart Line indicates emotional well-being and relationships, the Head Line reveals intellect and decision-making, and the Life Line reflects vitality and life changes.

The mounts (elevated areas of the palm) provide additional insights into personality traits and strengths.
